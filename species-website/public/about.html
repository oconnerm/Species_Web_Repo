Education and outreach:

If you’re interested in using this app as an informal learning tool with students of any age, or as part of a citizen science project, please contact Niwot Ridge LTER Education and Outreach Coordinator Dr. Alexandra Rose (alexandra.rose@colorado.edu) for supporting resources and to let us know how you’re using the app.

Species Information

The species in this app are a representative, but not exhaustive, list of species growing at the University of Colorado’s Mountain Research Station and Niwot Ridge. For more information regarding the species or their attributes, please contact Dr. Jane Smith (jane.g.smith@colorado.edu).
